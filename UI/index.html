Welcome to Politico

Politico is a platform designed for both the politicans and citizens. Politico enables citizens give their mandate to politicians running for different government offices while building trust in the process through tranparency.

Who Can Register

This platform is open to all citizens to use. Politicians can register to indicate interest in running for government offices. Likewise, citizens can register to decide who gets their mandate in the electoral process.

What We Can Offer

You get to see who is contesting. The election result is opened to all users to see who win a particular government position. You get to see the lists of poitical parties available to participate in the electoral process.

What You Can Do

The electoral process is open and fair. When politician who contested for a government position is not happy with the outcome of the election, he/she can decide to raise a petition against the result.
